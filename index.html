Methodology: Each game runs 10,000 Monte Carlo iterations. Per iteration: (1) draw a shared efficiency shock that correlates both teams' scoring, (2) generate possessions from a pace prior (8–17 drives), (3) draw per-drive outcomes from {TD, FG, none} using a 60/40 blend of market-implied and team-stat priors, (4) overlay Poisson-drawn turnovers (~4-pt swing per net TO) + weather/HFA/rest adjustments.
